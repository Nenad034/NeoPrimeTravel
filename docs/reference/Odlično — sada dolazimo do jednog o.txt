Odlično — sada dolazimo do jednog od **najkritičnijih modula cele platforme**: **Product & Contracting modul**.
Ovo je modul gde se upravlja:

* hotelima
* ugovorima sa dobavljačima
* cenovnicima
* sezonama
* doplatama i popustima
* stop-sale pravilima
* minimum stay pravilima
* allotmentima

Drugim rečima, ovo je **sistem koji upravlja inventory-jem i cenama** koje se kasnije koriste u:

* pretrazi
* paketiranju
* rezervacijama
* finansijama
* operativi

---

# 1. Svrha Product & Contracting modula

Ovaj modul mora omogućiti:

1. upravljanje hotelima i drugim proizvodima
2. unos ugovora sa dobavljačima
3. upravljanje cenama po sezoni
4. upravljanje doplatama i popustima
5. upravljanje allotmentima
6. upravljanje pravilima prodaje

Sve to mora biti fleksibilno jer u turizmu postoji ogromna varijacija.

---

# 3. Product modul

Product je osnovni entitet.

Primeri proizvoda:

* hotel
* cruise
* excursion
* transfer
* tour program
* ticket
* insurance

Za hotel:

### osnovni podaci

* product id
* name
* destination
* address
* category
* stars
* supplier
* latitude
* longitude
* description
* facilities

---

# 4. Room types

Hotel mora imati room types.

Primer:

```text
Standard Room
Deluxe Room
Family Room
Suite
```

Svaki room type mora imati:

* room code
* max occupancy
* base occupancy
* extra bed allowed
* baby cot allowed

---

# 5. Board plans

Board plan je način ishrane.

Primeri:

* RO (room only)
* BB (bed & breakfast)
* HB (half board)
* FB (full board)
* AI (all inclusive)

Board plan mora biti povezan sa cenama i doplatama.

---

# 6. Contract modul

Contract predstavlja ugovor sa dobavljačem.

Primer:

```text
Supplier: Sea Star Hotel
Contract period: 01.05 – 31.10
Currency: EUR
Market: Serbia
```

Polja ugovora:

* supplier
* product
* contract period
* currency
* payment terms
* cancellation policy
* release period
* notes

---

# 7. Seasons

Cene u turizmu se menjaju po sezonama.

Primer:

```text
Low season
01.05 – 31.05

Mid season
01.06 – 30.06

High season
01.07 – 31.08
```

Svaka sezona ima:

* start date
* end date
* season name
* priority

---

# 8. Rates (cene)

Rates su cene po sobi, po noći ili po osobi.

Primer:

```text
Room: Deluxe
Board: BB
Season: High
Price: 120 €
```

Cene mogu biti:

* per room
* per person
* per adult
* per child

---

# 9. Occupancy pricing

Cena može zavisiti od occupancy.

Primer:

```text
1/1 → 120 €
1/2 → 90 €
2/2 → 90 €
extra adult → 70 €
child → 50 €
infant → free
```

Sistem mora podržati sve ove kombinacije.

---

# 10. Supplements

Supplements su doplate.

Primeri:

* sea view
* balcony
* extra bed
* baby cot
* dinner supplement
* festive dinner
* airport transfer

Primer doplate:

```text
Sea view supplement
+20 € per room per night
```

---

# 11. Mandatory supplements

Neke doplate su obavezne.

Primer:

* New Year dinner
* Christmas dinner
* resort fee
* city tax

Primer:

```text
Mandatory gala dinner
31.12
80 € per person
```

---

# 12. Discounts

Popusti mogu biti:

* early booking
* long stay
* last minute
* free nights
* child discount
* senior discount

Primer:

```text
Early booking
10% discount
valid until 31.03
```

---

# 13. Free nights

Tipična turistička promocija.

Primer:

```text
Stay 7 nights
Pay 6 nights
```

ili

```text
Stay 10
Pay 8
```

---

# 14. Child policy

Child policy je važna za hotel pricing.

Primer:

```text
Child 0–2 → free
Child 3–12 → 50%
Child 13+ → adult
```

---

# 15. Minimum stay

Hotel može zahtevati minimalan broj noći.

Primer:

```text
High season
minimum stay 5 nights
```

---

# 16. Stop sale

Hotel može zatvoriti prodaju.

Primer:

```text
Stop sale
10–15 July
```

---

# 17. CTA / CTD

CTA = closed to arrival
CTD = closed to departure

Primer:

```text
CTA 01–05 August
```

Znači gost ne može početi boravak tih dana.

---

# 18. Release period

Release je rok do kada sobe moraju biti potvrđene.

Primer:

```text
Release: 7 days
```

Ako rezervacija nije potvrđena do tada:

* hotel može vratiti sobu u prodaju.
